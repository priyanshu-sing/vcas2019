2ndVCAS 2019

2ndInternational Conference on VLSI, Communication and Signal Processing

Organised by ECED MNNIT Allahabad
November 29 to December 1, 2019

About

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology, proudly announces the hosting of First International Conference on VLSI, Communication and Signal Processing (VCAS 2019). The conference aims to provide a platform for academicians, individuals working in the industry, as well as students to showcase their research innovations and get recognition. VCAS 2019 aspires to bring together research professional fraternity for the discussions and exchange of information on advances in VLSI, Signal Processing and Communications.

In a quest to regard all classes of research-oriented audience from across the globe, the three-day conference (VCAS 2019) is proposed to be held from November 29, 2019 to December 1, 2019. The scope of the conference embraces all the latest research trends in Electronics and Communication Engineering.
